1. The drafted cohort definition (inclusion criteria, exclusion criteria, index event, etc.)
2. EHR catalog verification results for each criterion showing whether matching concepts exist in the data

A. DOMAIN AVAILABILITY
   - Is the required clinical domain (conditions, drugs, procedures, measurements, visits) present in the EHR?

B. VOCABULARY COVERAGE

C. DATA COMPLETENESS
   - How many patients have the relevant data?
   - Is the coverage sufficient for the study?

D. TEMPORAL FEASIBILITY
   - Does the date range of the data support the required temporal windows?

- "NOT_SUPPORTED": The criterion cannot be implemented with available data
